Unity Candle

Meaning: A Unity Candle ceremony is a symbolic ritual often performed during wedding ceremonies. It involves the lighting of two individual candles by representatives from each side of the couple's families, typically the mothers. These two individual candles represent the separate lives, families, and experiences of the bride and groom leading up to their wedding day. Learn more

Cord of Unity

Meaning: As the couple exchanges vows or makes promises to each other, the cord becomes a tangible representation of the promises being woven into the fabric of their shared life. The Cord of Unity ceremony is a visual and meaningful way to express the idea that the couple is now bound together in love and commitment, embarking on a journey of shared experiences and mutual support. Learn more

Vow & Ring Exchange

Meaning: In the exchange of vows, personal promises of love and support are expressed, serving as heartfelt declarations of their intentions for marriage. Simultaneously, the exchange of rings symbolizes an eternal commitment, as the couple gives and receives bands representing enduring love and a pledge to remain together through life's joys and challenges. Closing
